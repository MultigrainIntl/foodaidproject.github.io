Our commitment

How we account for your support

Food Aid Project is built around efficiency — moving bulk food at the lowest possible cost so more reaches people in need. We hold ourselves to that same standard in how we report our work: we publish figures only when they're verified, we label projections and capacity estimates as such, and we never present a goal as an achieved result. If you have a question about our status, finances, or programs, we want to answer it.
